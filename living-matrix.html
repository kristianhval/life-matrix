☀ How to Use the Life Matrix Organizer
⌄
  1. Set your Period (any date range: one week, two weeks, a full month) and a Theme to anchor your focus for the period.
  2. Name each section with an area of your life. Use ♥ Save Template to lock in your section names and ↺ Load Template to restore them next period.
  3. Drag and drop tasks between sections if anything lands in the wrong place.
  4. Star ★ the most important item in each section. Pick your single ONE Thing across all sections and block time for it first.
  5. Add appointments with a date so they appear on the monthly calendar. Hover a gold dot to see what is scheduled that day.
  6. At the end of your period, click ✓ Close Out Week. Carry forward, drop, or delegate each incomplete task. History saves automatically.
  7. Life is a balancing act, not a balanced state. The radar shows where your energy is going. Imbalance chosen consciously is strategy.
